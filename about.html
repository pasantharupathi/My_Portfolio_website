About Me

Hi, I’m Pasan Tharupathi, a passionate tech enthusiast and aspiring programmer currently studying at the University of Westminster. I have a deep love for technology and creativity, and I enjoy building digital solutions that make life simpler, smarter, and more engaging. Whether it’s designing a modern interface or writing efficient code, I’m always driven by curiosity and the desire to learn something new every day.


Over the past few years, I’ve been developing my skills in web development, programming, and UI/UX design, exploring how creativity and logic come together to solve real-world problems. My time working at Dialog Finance gave me valuable experience in communication and teamwork — helping me understand the importance of collaboration, user satisfaction, and adaptability in a fast-moving environment


Beyond coding, I’m someone who loves music, exploring ideas, and continuously improving myself. I believe every project is a new opportunity to grow, connect, and make a positive impact through technology. My ultimate goal is to become a professional developer who not only builds software but also inspires innovation and creativity in others.
